# BetixPro: The Ultimate User Guide

Welcome to **BetixPro**! This guide is designed to help you navigate our platform with ease, from your very first login to withdrawing your winnings. Follow these steps to make the most of your betting experience.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Creating Your Account
1.  Click the **"Sign Up"** button on the home page.
2.  Enter a valid **Email Address** and a strong **Password**.
3.  Provide your **Full Name** and **Phone Number** (ensure this matches your mobile money registered number).
4.  Once registered, you may be required to verify your email or phone number to unlock all platform features.

### 1.2 Accessing Your Profile
Your profile is your personal hub. Click on your profile icon to:
*   Update your personal information.
*   Monitor your account status.
*   View your detailed betting and transaction history.
*   Enable security features like Two-Factor Authentication (if available).

---

## 2. Managing Your Funds

### 2.1 Depositing Funds (Adding Balance)
To start betting, you need to add funds to your BetixPro wallet.
1.  Navigate to the **"Deposit"** section in your dashboard.
2.  **M-Pesa (STK Push)**:
    *   Enter the amount you wish to deposit (Minimum: 100 KES).
    *   Confirm your phone number.
    *   Wait for a prompt on your phone, enter your M-Pesa PIN, and confirm.
    *   Funds will be credited to your wallet **instantly**.
3.  **Paystack**:
    *   Select Paystack to use your Credit/Debit card or Bank transfer.
    *   Follow the secure on-screen instructions to complete the payment.

### 2.2 Withdrawing Your Winnings
1.  Go to the **"Withdraw"** section.
2.  Enter the amount to withdraw (Minimum: 100 KES).
3.  **Important Note**: A **5% withdrawal fee** applies to all requests.
4.  Requests are typically reviewed within 24 hours. Once approved, the funds will be sent directly to your registered M-Pesa number.

## 3. The Betting Experience

### 3.1 Exploring Sports & Events
Use the navigation bar to browse through various sport categories like **Football**, **Basketball**, and **Tennis**.
*   **Featured Events**: Top matches hand-picked for our users.
*   **Upcoming**: Matches scheduled to start soon.
*   **Live**: Ongoing matches where you can bet in real-time as the action unfolds.

### 3.2 Understanding Odds
BetixPro uses **Decimal Odds**. To calculate your potential payout:
> **Potential Payout = Stake × Odds**
*Example: A 1,000 KES stake on 2.50 odds results in a 2,500 KES payout.*

### 3.3 Placing a Bet
1.  Find the match and outcome you want to bet on.
2.  Click on the **Odds** to add the selection to your **Bet Slip**.
3.  In the Bet Slip:
    *   **Single Bet**: Bet on one outcome.
    *   **Combo/Normal Bet**: Combine multiple selections for higher potential winnings (odds are multiplied).
4.  Enter your **Stake** amount.
5.  Review your "Potential Payout" and click **"Place Bet"**.

## 4. Tracking & Social Features

### 4.1 "My Bets" Section
Monitor your progress in the **"My Bets"** section:
*   **Active**: Bets currently in progress.
*   **Settled**: Past bets that have been marked as Won, Lost, or Void.
*   **Details**: Click on any bet to see the full selection breakdown and result status.

### 4.2 Sharing Your Bets
Found a great match?
1.  Click the **"Share"** icon on any match card.
2.  Use your device's native sharing menu to send the link via WhatsApp, Telegram, SMS, or other social apps.
3.  Your friends can click the link to see the same match and join the action.
